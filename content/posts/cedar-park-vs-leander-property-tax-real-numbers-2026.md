# Cedar Park vs Leander Property Tax Rate 2026: What a $450K Home Actually Costs You

If you're shopping for a home in the Austin suburbs and narrowing it down to **Cedar Park or Leander**, the property tax difference isn't just a percentage point — it's real money every month.

Here's exactly what you'll pay in 2026, broken down by city, with no fluff.

---

## 2026 Property Tax Rates at a Glance

| City | Combined Tax Rate (est.) |
|------|--------------------------|
| **Cedar Park** | ~2.10% – 2.25% |
| **Leander** | ~2.20% – 2.40% |

These rates include city, county, school district (Leander ISD covers both), and any applicable MUD or special district taxes.

> **Note:** Your exact rate depends on which subdivision you're in. MUD districts in Leander can add 0.10% – 0.40% on top of the base rate. Always verify with the Williamson Central Appraisal District (WCAD).

## Real Dollar Comparison on a $450,000 Home

After applying the standard **homestead exemption** (which removes $100,000 from your assessed value), your taxable value drops to $350,000.

| City | Taxable Value | Rate | Annual Tax | Monthly |
|------|--------------|------|------------|---------|
| **Cedar Park** | $350,000 | 2.15% | **$7,525** | **$627** |
| **Leander (no MUD)** | $350,000 | 2.25% | **$7,875** | **$656** |
| **Leander (with MUD)** | $350,000 | 2.55% | **$8,925** | **$744** |

**Takeaway:** Cedar Park typically runs $350–$1,400/year cheaper on a $450K home, depending on MUD status.

---

## Why Leander Rates Can Run Higher

Leander has grown fast — and that growth comes with infrastructure costs. Several newer subdivisions (Crystal Falls, Bryson, Northline, Deerbrooke) were developed using **Municipal Utility Districts (MUDs)**, which carry their own tax rate on top of everything else.

Once MUD bonds are paid off (typically 15–25 years), those rates drop. But if you're buying into a newer community today, you're likely paying that premium right now.

---

## Where Cedar Park Saves You Money

Cedar Park's older, more established infrastructure means fewer active MUD districts. Areas like **Block House Creek, Buttercup Creek, and Twin Creeks** often carry cleaner tax rates without the MUD surcharge.

That said, Cedar Park isn't uniformly cheaper — some pockets along 183A and newer developments near Leander's border carry similar rates.

## What About the Homestead Exemption?

Texas homeowners who occupy their home as a primary residence qualify for a **$100,000 homestead exemption** (as of 2023 legislation). That means:

- You're taxed on $350K, not $450K
- On a 2.25% rate, that saves you roughly **$2,250/year**
- File with WCAD within your first year — don't miss it

## Bottom Line: Which City Is Cheaper?

**Cedar Park edges out Leander** on base tax rates in most comparisons — but the margin narrows or reverses depending on MUD status.
