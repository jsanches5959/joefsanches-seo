## Navigating the Buying Process: Tips from a Military Veteran Realtor

Buying a newly constructed home is different from purchasing a resale property. Here are some veteran-tested tips to ensure you make a confident decision:

### 1. Research Builder Reputation

Before committing, check builder reviews in Leander and visit completed homes to assess quality.

### 2. Understand the Timeline

New construction often involves waiting for permits, construction, and inspections. Plan your move-out and finances accordingly.

### 3. Leverage Your Military Benefits

Leander resides in Williamson County—or parts in Travis County—so be prepared for annual taxes around 2.2% to 2.5% of the home’s value. Most new communities have HOA fees averaging $300 to $600 annually.
