## Commuting from Leander: Work Proximity and Transportation

One of the biggest considerations when relocating to Leander for work housing is the daily commute.

### Driving and Traffic

- Leander is approximately 25 miles from downtown Austin via US-183 and I-35.
- Typical commute times vary but are manageable compared to living further north or west.
- The toll road 183A offers a quicker route with less congestion.

### Public Transit Options

- The Capital MetroRail Red Line connects Leander directly to downtown Austin, providing an excellent alternative to driving.
- Park-and-ride facilities near Leander Station make it convenient to combine driving and rail transit.
