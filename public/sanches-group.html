Property Management Partners

Your vendor. Not your competition.

Sanches Group provides trades and leasing services to property management companies as an outside contractor. We don't manage properties, and we don't solicit your owners or tenants.

How it works for PM companies

You keep the owner relationship and the management contract. We handle the work orders — licensed, responsive, and accountable to you.

When a unit needs a plumber at 7am, a vacant property needs a turnaround before new tenants, or you need an electrician for a commercial unit — you call us. We show up, we invoice clearly, and we don't talk to your clients about anything else.

No upselling your owners. No poaching. Just reliable trades work that makes your operation smoother.

Discuss a Partnership

What PM partners get

  • Licensed plumbing, electrical, and GC work — one vendor, all trades
  • Fast turnaround on unit turns and vacancy prep
  • Clear invoicing — line-item, no surprises, no unauthorized markups
  • Leasing support for hard-to-fill units
  • A licensed, insured team that shows up and communicates
  • Real estate division handles individual buyer/seller transactions only — never your managed portfolios
  • Referral arrangement available for leasing and property-ready services
