## HUB Coordinator Contact Sources

To find HUB coordinators at Texas agencies:

1. **Texas Comptroller HUB Coordinator List:** comptroller.texas.gov/purchasing/vendor/hub/ → "HUB Coordinators"
2. **Individual agency procurement pages:**
   - TxDOT: txdot.gov → Business → Procurement
   - TPWD: tpwd.texas.gov → Business → Purchasing
   - HHSC: hhs.texas.gov → Business → Procurement
3. **Texas SmartBuy:** esbd.texas.gov — search agency contact lists
4. **Call the agency switchboard** and ask for the HUB coordinator directly — name and email

---

## Best Practices for Outreach

- Send with **capability statement attached** (see docs/capability-statement.md)
- **Follow up by phone** 2–3 business days after email — HUB coordinators receive many emails
- Ask: "Do you have any upcoming spot purchases or maintenance needs where a VetHUB vendor would be appropriate?"
- Note your **B2G VID** in every communication — it makes lookup fast for coordinators
- **Register on Texas SmartBuy** (esbd.texas.gov) so agencies can see you in vendor searches
