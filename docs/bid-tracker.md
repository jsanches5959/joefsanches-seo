## Notes / Lessons Learned

- Track bid-to-win ratio after 6 months: target ≥ 30% win rate on submitted bids
- For TPWD: contacts respond faster by phone than email
- Spot purchases (<$25K): often no formal bid process — just a quote request to vendors in the CMBL database for that NIGP code; updating CMBL codes is critical to receiving these
- End-of-fiscal-year (August in Texas): agencies often rush to spend remaining budget — increase monitoring in July/August
